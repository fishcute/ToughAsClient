# Thermometer

The thermometer will show your [immediate temperature](https://github.com/fishcute/ToughAsClient/blob/main/Tutorial/Stats/Immediate%20Temperature.md).

Possible temperatures:
- Freezing
- Cold
- Cool
- Mild
- Warm
- Hot
- Sweltering
